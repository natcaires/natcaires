Target Audience

The target audience for this website is people aging between 18 to 35 years who are either stay-at-home parents, college students or full time employees. They need a trustworthy forecat site to know how they need to prepare for the day and the whole week.

Personas

Persona 1

Group: Stay-at-home Parent

Demographics: Female, 25 year-old, Stay-at-home Mom.

Goals: She needs to take her kindergartener son, to school everyday. She practices exercises at home. She studies online.

Environment:She preferes using her smartphone to use the internet since it is the only device she can take everywhere, but she is confortable with desktops as well.

Persona 2

Group: Full Time employee

Demographics: Male, 28 year-old, Software Engineer.

Goals: He lives uptown and works downtown. He drives to work everyday.

Environment: As a Software Engineer he is confortable whith any kind of device. But as a full time employee he spends most of his time using desktops.
