Contrast

Semana Omnistack

rocketseat.com.br/week-10/inscricao

It is important that your website content has contrast with its background. This way the user will be able easily see and read everything on the webpage, so he will feel confortable while using your it and have a good experience with it, making him want to stay and check everything that he is interested in your website. The light colors chosen for the fonts and buttons on this website contrast perfectly with the dark background. Some of the letters are white, which obviously contrast very well with a dark background, but this website shows us a perfect example that we do not need to use only white in this case, any light colors would go well in this design, just like the light green the designer chose to build the website's identity.

Repetition shows that all the elements in your page belong to the same one thing. Like in this example from the Twitter website. They repeated the fonts and colors, making everything look like the belong together, that they are part of the same thing, the same subject, the same company.
