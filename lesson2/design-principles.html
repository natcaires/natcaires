Contrast

Semana Omnistack

rocketseat.com.br/week-10/inscricao

It is important that your website content has contrast with its background. This way the user will be able easily see and read everything on the webpage, so he will feel confortable while using it and have a good experience with it, making him want to stay and check everything that he is interested in your website.

Repetiton

Twitter

twitter.com

Repetition shows that all the elements in your page belong to the same one thing. Like in this example from the Twitter website. They repeated the fonts and colors, making everything look like the belong together, that they are part of the same thing, the same subject, the same company.

Proximity

Netflix

netflix.com

Here is a good example of correct Proximity in a website. We can see that the elements that belong to the same subject are very close to each other and a little far from elements that belong to another subject. This way we know exactly when a subject starts, when it ends and when another one starts. Making the website easy to read.
